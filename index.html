Our mission

Music with a purpose

Euphoria Fest 2027 is a charity music festival that targets Superfans aged 16–30 — digital natives who live on Instagram and TikTok. Every single ticket sold contributes directly to #SummerJam's fund for local hospitals and unemployment support.

We believe a global festival should cost no more than a cup of coffee. At €4 per ticket, the only barrier is not wanting to be there.

100% female line-up Online · Worldwide Charity fundraiser Superfans 16–30 June 27 – Jul 3, 2027

The #SummerJam movement

A clear, verified portion of every ticket sale goes to real humanitarian programs — local hospitals and unemployment support. Talking about a good cause on Instagram and TikTok is the most authentic form of marketing. We invite fans to share their #SummerJam moment and own the cause together.

The experience
More than just a stream

Euphoria Fest 2027 is engineered to make watching at home feel like being front-row. Three core interactive features transform passive viewers into active participants.

HD Live Stream

Crystal-clear high-definition streaming direct to your screen, with multi-angle camera selection for every performance. Zero buffering, zero compromise.

KPI impact: Core engagement rate · Replay views

Live Fan Chat

Real-time global chat rooms per artist. Connect with hundreds of thousands of fans during every performance — the social energy of a crowd, without the crowd.

KPI impact: Session duration · Social sharing rate

Song Voting

Vote for your favourite track in real time. The top-voted song gets added to each artist's encore set list — fans shape the show.

KPI impact: Conversion rate · Word-of-mouth referrals

Multi-language

Live subtitles and translations in 12 languages. No fan left behind anywhere in the world, making the festival truly global.

KPI impact: International ticket sales · Geographic reach

Exclusive Playlists

Unlock artist-curated pre-show playlists and access exclusive behind-the-scenes audio content — available only to ticket holders.

KPI impact: Ticket perceived value · Repeat purchase rate

Digital Merch

Limited-edition digital collectibles, festival badges and shareable frames per night attended. Your proof of being there — shareable on Instagram and TikTok.

KPI impact: Organic reach · Brand impressions
